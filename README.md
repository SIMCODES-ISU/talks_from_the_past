# Talks From The Past

This repository collects past talks given by both mentors and mentees as part
of the SIMCODES program. Talks includes traditional slides and posters.

Talks are organized by year and then by event. The events are:

- `bootcamp/`. This is the initial onboarding week of SIMCODES. In these folders
  you will find slides related to introductory topics in chemistry, computer
  science, and machine learning.
- `midterm_presentations/`. At about the six week mark mentees are asked to
  present their research so far. In these folders you will find slide decks
  written by the mentees describing the wonderful work they have done so far.
- `posters/`. The capstone of the SIMCODES program is sharing your research with
  the ISU community at the Summer Undergraduate Poster Symposium. In this
  directory you will find succinct summaries of what each mentee accomplished
  during the summer.
